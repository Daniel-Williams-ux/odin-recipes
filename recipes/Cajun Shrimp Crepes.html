Cajun Shrimp Crepes

Louisiana flavors get the ultimate crepe treatment in this perfectly seasoned recipe. Succulent shrimp, chopped bell peppers, and garlic make up the filling, while the entire crepe is slathered with a cream-based Cajun sauce.


Ingredients


Crepe Batter:

  • 1 cup milk
  • ⅔ cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 eggs
  • ½ teaspoon granulated garlic
  • 1 pinch salt

Filling:

  • ¼ cup butter
  • ½ cup chopped red bell pepper
  • ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning

Sauce:

  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons dry white wine

Steps


Step 1

  1. Combine milk, flour, and eggs in a blender.
  2. Blend on high speed until well mixed, about 1 minute.
  3. Let batter rest for 30 minutes.

Step 2

  1. Heat an 8-inch nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Lift pan and pour about 3 tablespoons batter into the center; tilt skillet so batter evenly coats bottom. Cook until top appears dry, 45 to 60 seconds.
  3. Flip and cook 15 to 20 seconds more. Remove crepe and stack onto a plate.
    4. Repeat with remaining batter; you will have leftover crepes for another use.

Step 3

  1. Melt 2 tablespoons butter for filling in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add bell peppers and onion; stir-fry until tender, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in garlic and cook until fragrant. Add remaining butter, shrimp, and Cajun seasoning.

Step 4

  1. Mix cream, flour, and Cajun seasoning together; slowly pour into the shrimp mixture.
  2. Cook and stir until slightly thickened, about 2 minutes.
  3. Pour in white wine and cook until heated through and thickened as desired, 2 to 5 minutes more.

Step 5

  1. Spoon some of the shrimp filling into the middle of each crepe and roll crepe around filling, or fold in halves or quarters.

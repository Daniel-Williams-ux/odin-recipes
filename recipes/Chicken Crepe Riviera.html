Chicken Crepe Riviera

We have a lovely restaurant nearby and my first-ever order was their chicken crêpe riviera. This is my version. The restaurant version doesn't have portobellos but I find they're a lovely addition and I love how easy it is to customize the flavors!


Ingredients


Basil Crepe Batter:

  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter, at room temperature
  • 6 leaves fresh basil leaves, minced
  • 1 pinch salt

Marinade:

  • 1 ½ pounds chicken tenderloins, diced
  • ¼ cup grapeseed oil
  • 1 ½ tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 pinch salt

Toppings:

  • 1 head broccoli, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 Roma tomatoes, chopped
  • ½ (8 ounce) package sliced portobello mushrooms (Optional)

Sauce:

  • ¾ cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons semi-soft cheese with garlic and herbs (such as Allouette®)
  • 1 tablespoon pesto

Steps


Step 1

  1. Combine milk, flour, and eggs in a blender. Blend until smooth, about 1 minute.
  2. Combine milk, flour, and eggs in a blender. Blend until smooth, about 1 minute.

Step 2

  1. Heat a nonstick pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Brush hot pan with a small amount of butter. Ladle about 3 tablespoons of batter into the center of the pan.
  3. Tilt pan from left to right to cover entire surface. Sprinkle top of the crepe with 1 or 2 teaspoons of basil.

Step 3

  1. Cook until edges begin to brown, 2 to 3 minutes.
  2. Flip crepe over and cook until set, about 2 minutes more.
  3. Remove to a plate. Cook remaining batter, re-brushing pan with butter and stacking crepes with parchment paper in between.

Step 4

  1. Mix chicken, grapeseed oil, balsamic vinegar, and salt together in a resealable zip-top bag; marinate for 20 to 30 minutes.

Step 5

  1. Drain and discard marinade. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add chicken; cook and stir until juices run clear, about 5 minutes.

Step 6

  1. Place broccoli in a microwave-safe bowl with 2 tablespoons water.
  2. Microwave until crisp-tender, 3 to 4 minutes.

Step 7

  1. Combine butter and garlic in the same skillet over medium heat.
  2. Cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the broccoli; stir to coat with the garlic butter. Stir in tomatoes and mushrooms and heat through.

Step 8

  1. Stir cream, cheese, and pesto together in a separate saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Cook and stir until sauce comes together, 3 to 5 minutes.

Step 9

  1. Lay a crepe on a plate, line chicken filling down the middle, and drizzle sauce on top.
  2. Fold crepe over burrito-style; spoon more chicken and sauce over top. Repeat with remaining crepes, filling, and sauce.
